Some Nature Pics

Hood

Beautiful winter hike, crunching snow and a frigid chill. Incredible view of Mount Hood through the trees

Olympic Lake

After failing to drive up the road to Olympic National Park (apparently it's dangerous to drive on frozen roads), we were able to find a wonderful hike up to a lake. The sea of endless trees was incredible on the way up and made it a hike to remember

The Gorge

Carved by the columbia river, The Gorge is one of the most beatiful areas I've come across in my travels. Endless waterfalls, hikes, and magnificent mountains in the distance are just some of the features of this beautiful landmark
